Proof of Impact Protocol (PoIP)

A decentralized engine to fund real-world impact through transparent crypto staking.

How It Works

Users stake tokens into a pooled treasury. The treasury generates yield. AI selects real-world bounties. Yield funds the bounties. Stakers get symbolic $GROW tokens as proof of their impact.
