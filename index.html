Proof of Impact Protocol

Stake to Make a Difference

Proof of Impact (PoIP) is a decentralized protocol that incentivizes positive social outcomes through impact bounties.

Stakeholders make a difference by staking cryptocurrency, helping to grow the PoIP Treasury. The yield from the Treasury is used to fund real-world impact bounties, promoting and tracking verified proof of impact.
