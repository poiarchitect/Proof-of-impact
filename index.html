Welcome to the Proof of Impact Protocol (PoIP)

PoIP is a global funding system that turns pooled staking into real-world aid.

Stake your funds to grow the PoIP treasury. You’ll get your full stake back and earn $GROW — a symbolic reward for helping the world.

All bounties are funded from treasury yield only, ensuring transparency and sustainability. The longer you stake, the more $GROW you receive.
